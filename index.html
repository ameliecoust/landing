1. Who are we

Hi, I'm Amélie the COO of En Voiture Paris, the best carpooling application in Paris. With a geolocation system, it is easy to detect users on your route and going in the same direction. If you want to take a traveler, you can enter your trip in advance to facilitate searches for profiles going in the same direction.

2. How to use our app

Register now and create your online profile. Then, indicate your areas of interest as well as your status: driver or passenger. If you choose to be a driver, you can tell us your journeys in advance to facilitate the connection with the profiles on your way.If you are passengers, let us know your position and the application will take care of finding you a driver
